Deep cleaning

IN THE ROOMS:

  • We dust the ceiling and walls (throughout the building)
  • We remove dust from vertical and horizontal surfaces and equipment
  • We wash floor lamps, chandeliers, sconces, cornices
  • Move light furniture and wash behind it
  • Dry and wet cleaning of the floor and plinth
  • Washing mirrors, glass surfaces
  • We wash windowsills, batteries, switches, doors

IN THE KITCHEN:

  • We degrease and wash the work surface, countertop, adjacent surface, table, chairs, sink, stove
  • We wipe the facade of the kitchen and the cabinets inside
  • We wash household appliances (outside and inside as needed)
  • We wash the floor, ceiling (if necessary)

IN THE BATHROOM:

  • We wash the tile, the ventilation grill
  • We clean and disinfect sanitary ware (toilet, bidet, bathtub, shower cabin, washbasin)
  • We wash and polish faucets and watering cans
